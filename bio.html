Bio

Hi there! I'm Zi, meaning purple or violet in Chinese. It sounds like 'Zee' in English. Currently I'm studying in Tsinghua University, Beijing for my Bachelor's degree. My major is Computer Science and Technology. In my junior year of college, I started to do research with Prof. Jun Zhu on machine learning. My first project is on gibbs sampling algorithm for correlated topic model. I learned a bunch of new stuff! These include how to use Vim, Bash, operate fourty machines, etc, besides our main topic of topic model. In the summer of 2013, I visited Prof. Fei Sha's lab, and joined an exciting project on computational auditory scene analysis.

If you're also interested in my early life or future plan, go on reading.

Early days and future plan

In the lunar year of monkey, 1992, I was born in a small town in Hubei Province where a company for national defense was located and my grandfather served as the director. My parents, both graduated from the same school, Harbin Institute of Technology, as my grandfather did, worked as engineers there. When I was about 2, my family started to move. I spent my childhood in Xi'an, Xiamen and my teenage days in Shanghai. I think I've inherited most of my family's gene for science and engineering. In high school, I had already started to do research and got acknowledged, which was a plus when applying for Tsinghua University, the so-called best and hardest-to-get-in college in China. I've been a clarinet player since elementary school. I joined Tsinghua Marching Band in 2010. Music composed most of my leisure time in college. Like most my schoolmates, I wear glasses probably due to a limited time for outdoor sport and long exposure to coursework and extracurriculum coursework. But I'm changing now! I go swimming weekly and do yoga. I'm also getting my driver license! I have a plan to travel around the world making the best use of my free time in the last summer vacation of college, please inform me if you're interested in having the trip with me!
