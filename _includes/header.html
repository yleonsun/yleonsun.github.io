Welcome!

My name is Zi WANG, and I am a PhD student at EECS, MIT and part of LIS at CSAIL. I am working with Prof. Stefanie Jegelka, Prof. Leslie Pack Kaelbling, and Prof. Tomás Lozano-Pérez on exciting projects about active learning and Bayesian optimization with an ultimate goal of embedding the learning abilities in real robots that can help with household chores.
